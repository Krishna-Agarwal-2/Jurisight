AGREEMENT FOR SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT AND MAINTENANCE

This Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of January 1, 2024, by and between:

XYZ Technologies Inc., a corporation duly organized and existing under the laws of California, with its principal office at 123 Tech Road, Silicon Valley, California ("Developer");

ABC Enterprises, a company duly organized and existing under the laws of New York, with its principal office at 456 Business Ave, Manhattan, New York ("Client").

Recitals
WHEREAS, the Developer is in the business of providing software development and maintenance services;
WHEREAS, the Client desires to engage the Developer to develop certain software solutions for the Client's business operations;
NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. SERVICES TO BE PROVIDED
The Developer agrees to provide the following services ("Services"):
1.1. Develop a custom Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system tailored to the Client's specifications.
1.2. Provide ongoing software maintenance and support for a period of 12 months following the delivery of the CRM system.
1.3. Perform quarterly updates and troubleshooting as necessary to maintain system performance.

2. PAYMENT AND FEES
2.1. The total fee for the development of the CRM system shall be $150,000, payable as follows:

$50,000 upon signing of this Agreement.

$50,000 upon completion of the first software prototype.

$50,000 upon delivery and final acceptance of the CRM system.

2.2. The Client agrees to pay the Developer an additional $10,000 for any modifications or enhancements requested by the Client outside the original scope of work.

3. TERM AND TERMINATION
3.1. The Agreement shall commence on January 1, 2024, and shall continue for a period of one year, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the terms hereof.
3.2. Either party may terminate this Agreement for any reason with 30 days' written notice to the other party.
3.3. Upon termination, the Client shall pay for all services rendered up to the date of termination.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
4.1. Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information shared during the course of this Agreement.
4.2. The obligations under this section shall survive termination of the Agreement.

5. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
5.1. Any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.
5.2. The arbitration shall be held in New York, New York.

6. GOVERNING LAW
6.1. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.

7. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR
7.1. The Developer is an independent contractor and not an employee of the Client.
7.2. The Developer shall have no authority to bind the Client in any manner.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto have executed this Agreement as of the date first above written.
